    • Strawberry Milkshake

    A picture of the strawberry milshake

    A strawberry milkshake is a classic, creamy beverage made by blending strawberries, milk, and ice cream until smooth. It is a popular and refreshing treat, often enjoyed as a dessert or a snack

    Ingredients

    • 3 cups vanilla ice cream (slightly softened)
    • 1 cup fresh or frozen chopped strawberries
    • Â½ to 1 cup whole milk (adjust for desired consistency)
    • Optional: 1 tbsp sugar or sweetener (adjust based on strawberry sweetness), whipped cream, and extra strawberries for garnish

    Steps

    1. Prepare the strawberries: Rinse and hull the fresh strawberries, then chop them into smaller pieces.
    2. Combine ingredients: Place the chopped strawberries, ice cream, and milk into a blender.
    3. Blend: Blend on high speed until the mixture is completely smooth and well combined.
    4. Serve: Pour the milkshake into tall, chilled glasses.
    5. Garnish: Top with whipped cream and a fresh strawberry, if desired, and serve immediately.
