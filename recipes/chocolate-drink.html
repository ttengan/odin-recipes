Chocolate Drink Recipe

A glass of chocolate drink

Achocolate drink is a popular beverage made from combining chocolate (either melted or as cocoa powder) with milk or water, and typically a sweetener. It can be served hot or cold and has various forms across different cultures worldwid

Chocolate drink gives you a warm and fuzzy feeling after a long and tiring day. It reminds you of mum's warm hands.Chocolate drink is also very nutritious as it is creamy. Take a go at this recipe and i promise you'll enjoy it

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups milk (whole or 2% recommended)
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • ½ teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Place a saucepan over medium-low heat and add the milk until steaming.
  2. Whisk in the cocoa powder, sugar, and salt until no clumps remain.
  3. Stir in the chocolate chips and continue whisking until completely melted.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Pour into mugs and serve immediately.
