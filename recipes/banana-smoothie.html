Banana Smoothie

The banana-smoothie

A basic banana smoothie can be made with just three ingredients: frozen bananas, milk, and Greek yogurt. The result is a simple, creamy, and healthy drink that can be enjoyed for breakfast or as a snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 frozen bananas, sliced
  • 1 cup milk (dairy, almond, oat, or other choice)
  • 1 cup plain or vanilla Greek yogurt

Steps

  1. Combine the frozen banana slices, milk, and Greek yogurt in a blender.
  2. Blend until the mixture is completely smooth and creamy.
  3. Serve immediately. If the smoothie is too thick, add a splash more milk and blend again
